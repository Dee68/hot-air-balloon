How We Began

Kerry Balloon Tours began with a simple dream and a deep love for the beautiful landscapes of County Kerry. A small group of adventure enthusiasts wanted to find a unique way for visitors and locals to experience the breathtaking scenery of the region—from the rolling green hills to the sparkling lakes and coastline. What started as a single balloon and a passion for flight quickly grew into something special. After the first few tours, word spread about the peaceful experience of floating high above the countryside while watching the sunrise over the mountains. Visitors from around Ireland and beyond began coming to Kerry to experience the magic of ballooning.
